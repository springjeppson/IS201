All About New Zealand

I consider New Zealand to be my second home. I spent 18 months living there, serving the beautiful people of New Zealand and fell absolutely in love with the people, the place, and the culture. As a missionary for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, my responsibility was to teach everyone that wanted to listen about Jesus Christ and how we can come unto Him through faith, repentance, and baptism. My life has been forever changed because of the lessons I learned and the experiences I had in New Zealand.

The Auckland, New Zealand Temple Open House



One of the biggest things happening in New Zealand right now is the open house of the Auckland Temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I lived less than 5 minutes away from this temple as it was being built. It is a majestic site and can be seen from all around, driving past on the motorway or from the neighborhoods behind it. Enjoy this video of the beautiful House of the Lord and a small glimpse of the beautiful people of New Zealand that will forever hold my heart.

Tokoroa



Tokoroa is a small town in the Waikato region of New Zealand. It is a beautiful place with a lot of nature and a lot of friendly people. I spent 6 months of my mission in Tokoroa and I have many special friendships from my time there. The people there welcomed me in as family and I can't wait to visit them again someday.

Tauranga



Tauranga is a beautiful city in the Bay of Plenty region of New Zealand. It is a place with a lot of beaches and a lot of sunshine. I spent the first 3 months of my mission in Tauranga and I learned so much about myself there, while enjoying the ocean air, the sound of waves, and the feel of sand beneath my feet.

South Auckland



South Auckland is a region of New Zealand that is known for its diversity and its culture. I spent 3 months of my mission in South Auckland and I learned so much about the people there and the way they live. My mind and heart was opened to the beautiful cultures that are found there.

Papakura



Papakura is a small town in the Auckland region of New Zealand. It is a place with a lot of history and a lot of charm. I spent the last 4 months of my mission in Papakura and I have many memories from my time there. The people there are so kind and so welcoming, offering help to any who may need it.

Hamilton Temple



The Hamilton New Zealand Temple is a popular landmark of the island country. It is a beautiful place where people can go to feel peace and to feel closer to God. I had the opportunity to visit the temple many times while I lived there.

Papakura Marae



A marae is a Maori meeting house where people can gather to celebrate, to mourn, and to learn. I had the opportunity to visit a marae while I lived in New Zealand and I learned so much about the Maori culture and the way they live. Wherever you are in New Zealand, you can find a local marae to visit.

Lake Maraetai



Lake Maraetai is a beautiful lake in the Waikato region of New Zealand. I had the opportunity to visit Lake Maraetai a few times while I lived in Tokoroa and would highly recommend going to visit it.

Huka Falls



Huka Falls is a popular tourist destination in New Zealand. It is a beautiful waterfall that is a must-see for anyone visiting the island country. I had the opportunity to visit Huka Falls while I lived in Tokoroa and I was amazed by the beauty of the falls.
